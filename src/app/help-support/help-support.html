Student Help Center

Getting Started

Learn the basics of using LearnHub as a student

How do I create a student account?
Click the "Sign Up" button, fill out your personal information, verify your email, and you'll have instant access to browse and book sessions.
How do I browse available sessions?
Go to the "Sessions" page where you can filter by category, date, time, and trainer. Use the search bar to find specific topics.
What information do I see about each session?
Each session shows the topic, trainer name, date/time, duration, available seats, category, and a brief description of what will be covered.

Booking Sessions

Everything about booking and managing your sessions

How do I book a session?
Click on any session, review the details, and click "Book Session" if seats are available. You'll receive a confirmation email with the meeting link.
Can I book overlapping sessions?
No, you cannot book sessions that overlap in time. You must cancel your existing booking first if you want to book a conflicting session.
What if a session is full?
You can join the waitlist. If someone cancels, you'll be automatically notified and given the opportunity to book the seat.

Managing Bookings

Cancel, reschedule, and track your bookings

How do I cancel a booking?
Go to your dashboard, find the session under "Upcoming Sessions," and click "Cancel." You must cancel at least 2 hours before the session starts.
What happens if I miss a session?
Missed sessions are marked as "No-Show" in your history. Multiple no-shows may result in booking restrictions. Contact support if you had an emergency.
Can I see my booking history?
Yes, your dashboard shows all past sessions with status (attended, cancelled, no-show) and any feedback you've provided.

Technical Issues

Troubleshooting common technical problems

I can't access the meeting link
Meeting links are sent via email after booking and are also available in your dashboard 30 minutes before the session. Check your spam folder or contact support.
What if I have audio/video issues during a session?
Try refreshing your browser, checking your microphone/camera permissions, or switching to a different browser. Use the session chat for help from the trainer.

Trainer Help Center

Becoming a Trainer

Apply and get approved as a LearnHub trainer

How do I apply to become a trainer?
Click "Become a Trainer," fill out the application form with your qualifications and experience. After submitting your details, there will be an interview and certificate verification process conducted outside of our website system before final approval by admin.
What qualifications do I need?
Requirements vary by category. Generally, you need relevant experience, certifications, or degrees in your field. Provide detailed information about your background during the application process.
How long does the approval process take?
The entire process including application review, interview, and verification typically takes 5-10 business days. You'll receive an email with the decision and next steps if approved.

Creating Sessions

Set up and manage your training sessions

How do I create a new session?
Go to your trainer dashboard, click "Create Session," fill in the topic, date/time, seat limit, category, and meeting link. Sessions need admin approval before going live.
Can I edit a session after creating it?
No, once a session is created and submitted for approval, trainers cannot edit the session details. Please ensure all information is accurate before submission.
How far in advance should I schedule sessions?
We recommend scheduling at least 48-72 hours in advance to allow for admin approval and student bookings. Popular times may need more lead time.

Managing Sessions

Handle your scheduled sessions effectively

How do I see who's registered for my sessions?
Your dashboard shows all upcoming sessions with the number of registered students. For privacy reasons, student contact information is not provided to trainers.
What if I need to cancel a session?
Once a session is approved by admin, trainers cannot cancel it. In case of emergencies, please contact admin support immediately for assistance.
How do I handle no-shows?
Wait 10-15 minutes past start time, then begin with present students. The system automatically marks absent students as no-shows.

Best Practices

Tips for successful training sessions

How do I ensure good student engagement?
Prepare interactive content, encourage questions, use polls or breakout rooms, and provide clear learning objectives at the start of each session.
What should I do if technical issues occur?
Have a backup plan, test your setup beforehand, and consider recording sessions when possible for makeup access if technical issues prevent the live session.

General Help

Account Management

Managing your LearnHub account settings

How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to your dashboard, click "Profile Settings," update your information, and save changes. Some changes may require email verification.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, go to Account Settings and click "Delete Account." This action is permanent and will cancel all upcoming bookings.

Platform Features

Understanding LearnHub's key features

What categories of learning are available?
We offer programming (C#, Java, HTML/CSS), professional skills, sports (Chess), languages, and more. Categories are managed by admins and regularly updated.
How does the rating system work?
After attending a session, you can rate the trainer and session quality. Ratings help other students choose and help us maintain quality standards.
